Biography

Leonardo da Vinci was a brilliant and versatile Italian artist, scientist, and inventor of the Renaissance period. He is best known for his paintings of the Mona Lisa and the Last Supper, which showcase his skillful use of light, perspective, and expression. He also made many discoveries and inventions in various fields, such as anatomy, mechanics, optics, and flight, but most of them remained unpublished and unknown in his lifetime.
